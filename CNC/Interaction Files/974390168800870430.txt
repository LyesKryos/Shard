The Empires of Almaaz, Bassiliya, and Shijeun agree to form the Southwestern Coalition. This coalition will agree to a pact of non-aggression, a pact of mutual defense, and a pact of non-interference. Each member will reject any offers of alliance until turn #150, at which time the terms of this coalition will be reassessed by all nations and any changes may be made. Any member who wishes to withdraw from the coalition will pay all signatories 5% of all their current treasury and notify the other members 4 turns in advance.
