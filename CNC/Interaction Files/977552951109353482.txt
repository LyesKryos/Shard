The Federation of Jaedonstan agrees to pay the nation of Hatariel 6000 and transfer the provinces of 104, 110, 116, 117, and 118 in exchange for the nation of Hatariel transferring the provinces of 41, 42, 43, 45, 36, 37, and 38 to the Federation of Jaedonstan. This exchange may take place at any time and in any combination of stipulaitons.
